Turn the Temperature On
and
the Cancer Off

This site includes the two documentes that were submitted by me and my team - SPECTech - in the international competition for High School physics students all over the World, the video presentation and the conclusion about the study.

Our proposal had the aim to observe the effect of temperature variations, at relatively extreme levels, on the trajectory, speed, and energy of protons to study the influence that these results may have in medical applications, specially related to cancer.
